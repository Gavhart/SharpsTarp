About SharpsTarps

Sharp’s Tarps LTD - A Legacy of Quality and Innovation

Founded in 1971, Sharp’s Tarps LTD is a testament to the dedication and entrepreneurial spirit of a family deeply rooted in the trucking industry. The company’s inception can be traced back to the experiences of its founder, Weston Sharp, a long-haul truck driver who faced recurrent challenges in sourcing quality tarps and obtaining necessary repairs while on the road. Frustrated by the lack of reliable services, Weston began a small-scale tarp repair operation right out of his family garage in Rogue River, Oregon.

The modest venture rapidly gained a reputation for meticulous workmanship and exceptional customer service, propelling Weston to transition from makeshift repairs to a full-scale tarp manufacturing business. Leveraging his intimate knowledge of the trucking industry’s needs, he began designing and producing tarps that not only met but exceeded the rigorous demands of daily freight operations. His endeavors were supported by his family, whose involvement was crucial in scaling the business operations.

By the mid-1970s, Sharp’s Tarps had established a significant presence along Interstate 5 and the Northern California coast, becoming synonymous with durability and reliability. The business expanded its services to include custom tarp solutions, catering to a diverse range of needs from weather-resistant covers to specialized industrial applications. In 1978, the growing demand led to the relocation of the company to a more substantial facility in Grants Pass, Oregon, where it continued to thrive and expand.

Following decades of successful leadership, Weston and his wife June retired, passing on the helm to their sons, Monte and Brett, who continue to lead the company forward into the new millennium. Under their management, Sharp’s Tarps has embraced modern technologies, incorporating advanced manufacturing techniques and sustainable materials, ensuring that the legacy of innovation and quality endures.

Today, Sharp’s Tarps remains a family-owned and operated enterprise, celebrated for its commitment to excellence and its profound impact on the trucking and freight industries. The company not only continues to serve as a leading provider of tarpaulin products but also as a cornerstone of the community in Grants Pass, contributing to local employment and participating actively in regional economic development.

As Sharp’s Tarps LTD. looks to the future, it remains dedicated to the principles of quality, innovation, and service that Weston Sharp envisioned over half a century ago, striving always to cover more than just loads but to safeguard a legacy of enduring values and dedicated service.
